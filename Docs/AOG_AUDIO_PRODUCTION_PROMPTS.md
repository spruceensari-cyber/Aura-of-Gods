# Aura of Gods — Audio Production Prompt Pack

These prompts are written for direct copy/paste into music, SFX and voice generation tools. Export clean WAV files when possible. Keep music loops seamless and avoid copyrighted melodies or imitations of identifiable artists/games.

Create a seamless 3-minute instrumental loop for a futuristic mythic competitive MOBA called Aura of Gods. The world feels like a divine metaverse 500 years in the future: obsidian temples, cyan energy rails, violet holographic runes, ancient gods rebuilt through impossible technology. Tempo 118 BPM. Hybrid orchestral and electronic score with deep taiko-like percussion, low synthetic pulses, distant choir textures without lyrics, evolving arpeggios, metallic impacts, wide cinematic bass, restrained brass, and luminous high-frequency details. The track must support 20- to 40-minute competitive play without becoming exhausting. Strong identity, no recognizable references to existing game music, no vocals, no lyrics, seamless loop ending.
